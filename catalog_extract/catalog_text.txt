ABOUT LEORA WELLNESS
 
Leora Wellness (Pvt) Ltd was established in December 2025 as a
dedicated personal care manufacturing company, built on a long-
standing passion for wellness and self-care products. Backed by
over 25 years of manufacturing experience through our parent
company Lanka Minerals and Chemicals (Pvt) Ltd, and as the
sister company of LFO Holdings (Pvt) Ltd, Leora focuses on
developing high-quality, innovative personal care products for
evolving market needs

Miracle Natural is a herbal-based personal care brand
developed under Leora Wellness (Pvt) Ltd, created with the
vision of making effective, nature-inspired products accessible to
everyone. Built on a strong manufacturing foundation, the brand
combines carefully selected herbal ingredients with modern
formulation expertise to deliver safe, affordable, and results-
driven products. Miracle Natural is designed for the everyday
consumer offering vibrant, approachable, and high-quality
personal care solutions that meet the needs of the mass market
without compromising on effectiveness.
